Overview

Rosalind is a site that was introduced to me by a close friend. He and I share an interest in bioinformatics, so I took to this quickly. I've been having quite a bit of fun with it, and hope to keep it that way. I started to become interested in this field during my time at UW, when I discovered that I much preferred my coursework in Genetics to just about anything else.

Technology

At the moment, this is written in nothing but pure Python 3, so it can't be run outside of a compiler or terminal. I'm currently in the bioinformatics stronghold, so expect the code to get more complex as time goes on. I will begin to include recursion as it becomes a greater part of repertoire, as well as additional helpful Python libraries (possibly including matplotlib and others in that vein).
